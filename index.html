Vision

We dream of an India where no child suffers.
No other country sees as many children die from preventable and treatable causes. Children in India need your help so they can have a healthy start in life.

  • 22% of people live in poverty
  • 38% of children suffer from stunting due to malnutrition
  • 1 child in 26 dies before their 5th birthday
  • 20% of school-age children are out of school
  • 12% of children are engaged in child labor
  • 1 in 40 girls between age 15-19 gives birth
  • 15% of girls age 15-19 are married
  • 39% of girls age 15 and older struggle to read or write
Children Are The Future of The Nation. Help Us To Save Our Future!










About Us


Although there is more wealth in India than ever before, it hasn’t fully translated into healthcare, education or better nutrition for the poor. Save Smiles is bringing healthcare to the impoverished areas of India, and we’re pioneering new ways of reaching communities to help treat malnutrition and prevent deaths.

We are still working to provide supplies like food, water and educational supplies to those in need. Although the floodwaters have receded, the damage and challenges that Indian children face will continue to be our priority – in times of crisis and every day.
